Choosing the Right Gold Purity for Affordable Yet Valuable Jewelry

Millennials seeking affordable gold jewelry often consider 18K or 22K options. While 24K is pure gold, it is softer and more expensive. Umar Jewellers advises opting for 22K gold for a good balance between purity, durability, and price—ideal for jewelry pieces that can be worn daily without wear and tear.
